How to Play — Rules & Scoring
▼
1
Create an account with your email. Your email auto-generates your team name — [email protected] becomes "Jake's Team". You can create multiple teams.
2
Pick 16 teams from all 68 NCAA Tournament teams across four regions (East, West, South, Midwest). You must select exactly 16. Play-in game teams are available to pick — their stats count from the first tip-off.
3
Pick 1 player per team. That player earns you fantasy points based on their real stats every time their team plays in the tournament. If their team gets eliminated, they stop scoring — but all points already earned stay.
4
Fantasy scoring: +1 point for every Point, Assist, Rebound, Block, and Steal your player records. −1 point for every Foul and Turnover. Points accumulate across the entire tournament.
5
Tiebreaker: When locking in your team you'll guess the total combined points scored in the April 6 National Championship game. If two entries tie on fantasy points, whoever guessed closest wins.
⏰
Picks lock at 12:15 PM ET on Thursday March 19 — tip-off of the first Round of 64 game (Ohio State vs TCU). You can update your player picks any time before that deadline, but your 16 teams are locked once submitted. Play-in games on March 17–18 count, so pick wisely!
